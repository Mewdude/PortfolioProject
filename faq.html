Frequently Asked Questions

Using the Specs Page

How do I find my product?

On the Specs Page, simply type your product code into the search bar and it will display your information

My product isn't shown, what do I do?

If your product code is not available, please submit a Bug Report Here and our team will work immediately to add it to your repository

The pictures associated with my product are outdated / incorrect

If your product information needs to be updated, please sumbmit the relevant pictures and documentation to our team at BugReport@QAHelper.com or by submitting a Bug Report

My issue is not listed here, who can I talk to?

If you need additional support, or the answer to your question is not here, please feel free to contact our team at: BugReport@QAHelper.com

Filing A Bug Report

I'm not an operator, what machine / shift should I use?

Even if you are not an operator, please enter the current shift and the machine that the product in question was run on to better help us identify and fix your problem.

I found a bug on my shift, but I'm pretty sure it happened on a different shift. What should I do?

If you encounter a bug that should have been addressed on a previous shift, please note that in the bug description. You should still press the relevant radio button for your shift on the form.

Why do I need to supply my phone number?

Sometimes, our Customer Service team may need more information related to your problem. If that happens, we will attempt to contact you via your email and phone number. Providing this information ensures a more timely response to your report.

My issue is not listed here, who can I talk to?

If you need additional support, or the answer to your question is not here, please feel free to contact our team at: BugReport@QAHelper.com
